By Mygel Bergstresser at Aug 23, 2016

This website was originally built to start a career as a web developer. Many redesigns later it turned into a web portfolio, but with one page dedicated to express my observations. Social media is an echo chamber of: memes, motivational quotes, passive aggressive statements, and squabbling over petty events when in reality there are larger issues that we need solutions for. This little page on the internet is a place to collect my thoughts and share my story.

My name is Mygel Bergstresser and I am a 25 year old who is dedicated to finding truth, happiness, and making this planet a little better than how I found it. I am not an activist looking to spread an agenda by painting life black or white. I am not a supporter of violence, harassment, and belittling one another. I am not an Elon Musk on the verge of pushing mankind into the next phase of civilization; don’t get me wrong I would love to! I am just a guy floating through this experience we call life. Speaking of life…

 

What’s with the name?

On June 30th 1991, Mount Pinatubo erupted in the Philippines. It killed over 700 people, lowered the global temperature by 33.08 degrees Fahrenheit, and the entire island tried to evacuate as the world watched in awe. During labor one of the delivery nurses asked my mother to name me after her son which was named Mygel. Pretty cool huh?
